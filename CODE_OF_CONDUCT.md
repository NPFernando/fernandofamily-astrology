# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be a welcoming, harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience
level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Assume good faith; disagree about code and ideas, not people.
- Accept feedback gracefully, and give it kindly.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language or jokes, and any
conduct that would reasonably make another contributor feel unsafe or
unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer via a
GitHub issue (for non-sensitive matters) or privately (see
[`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) for private-contact guidance, which also
applies to conduct reports of a sensitive nature). Maintainers may remove,
edit, or reject contributions, comments, and other interactions that don't
align with this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban
contributors for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.
